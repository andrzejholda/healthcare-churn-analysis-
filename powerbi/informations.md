# Power BI Dashboard Description

## Page 1 – Patient Retention & Revenue Impact Analysis
Purpose:
Provide a high-level view of clinic performance.

Key metrics:
- Total Revenue
- Unique Patients (active patients)
- Average Revenue per Patient
- Average Patients per Month

Business questions answered:
- Which clinics generate the highest revenue?
- How patient volume changes over time?
- Which procedures drive the most revenue?
- which group of age generate the most revenue ?
---

## Page 2 – Customer Churn & Revenue Loss Analysis
Purpose:
Identify where and why patients churn.

Key metrics:
- Churned Patients
- Churn Rate %
- Churn Revenue
- Churn Revenue Rate %

Business questions answered:
- Which clinics lose the most revenue due to churn?
- Which procedures are most churn-prone?
- Are there seasonal churn patterns?

---

## Page 3 – Retention Campaign Impact Simulator (SMS)
Purpose:
Estimate financial impact of a retention campaign.

Key metrics:
- Churn Revenue
- Recovered Revenue
- Net Revenue Gain
- ROI
- SMS Campaign Cost

Interactive elements:
- Recovery rate  (10–20%)

Business questions answered:
- Is the campaign profitable?
- At what recovery rate does it break even?
- Which clinics benefit the most?
