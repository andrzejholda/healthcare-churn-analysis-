# DAX Measures Overview

## This folder contains all DAX measures used in Power BI dashboards:

#### 1. churn_measures.dax
   - Churned Patients
   - Total Patients
   - Churn Rate %

#### 2. revenue_measures.dax
   - Churn Revenue
   - Total Revenue
   - Churn Revenue %
    
#### 3. cohort_measures.dax
   - Retention 3M
   - Retention 6M
   - Retention 12M
   - Cumulative revenue
   - Total cohort revenue

#### 4. campaign_simulation.dax
   - Recovery Rate (what-if parameter)
   - SMS Campaign Cost
   - Recovered Revenue
   - Net Revenue Gain
   - ROI
